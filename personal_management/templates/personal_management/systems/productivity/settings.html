Productivity System Setup

Activate the execution stack that keeps you focused: timers, reviews, blocking rules, and timeboxing tools.

  • Pomodoro Timer — set your preferred focus/break cadence and save defaults.
  • Blocking apps & sites — maintain a list of distractions to import into system-level blockers.
  • Weekly & Monthly Reviews — schedule recurring deep-dives to keep lessons and wins front of mind.
  • Big 4 Goals — flag the annual goals that matter most so they surface inside every review.
  • Habit Tracking — sync Body + Productivity habits and track completions each day.
  • Backward Engineering Tool — map target dates into milestones and work sessions.
  • Parkinson Law Tool — timebox tasks aggressively to prevent scope creep.

Everything you capture inside these dashboards lives locally until you sync with your knowledge or task system. The intent: fast capture, zero friction, relentless follow-through.
